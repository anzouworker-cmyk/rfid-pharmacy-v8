Smart Inventory V39 - Powerful local RFID agent

New AI Agent capabilities:
- Works locally without paid API.
- Lists missing/absent products directly from Catalogue + Associations + EPC detected CSV.
- Understands requests like: "montre-moi les 20 produits absents".
- Filters product lists by detected zone/category/name when the values exist in imported data.
- Generates an action plan based on missing, non-associated and duplicate EPC data.
- Exports CSV reports directly from the chat.
- Exports a simple PDF summary directly from the chat.
- Detects duplicate EPCs in the associations table.
- Explains why a product is "Présent", "Manquant" or "Non associé" using PID, barcode or product name.
- Adds quick action buttons inside the Assistant IA page: CSV manquants, PDF résumé, Doublons EPC.

Logic reminder:
- Présent = product has an associated EPC and this EPC is present in the imported EPC detected CSV.
- Manquant = product has an associated EPC but this EPC is absent from the imported EPC detected CSV.
- Non associé = product has no associated EPC.

Validation:
- Frontend build passed with `npm run build`.
