V60 - Admin-configurable cash reserve amount

Changes:
- Replaced the fixed 2000 DH rule with an admin-configurable reserve amount.
- Added cash settings storage for the reserve amount kept in caisse.
- The "À retirer" card now calculates automatically using:
  S. caisse (compté) - montant minimum à garder en caisse.
- Only admin users can open the "À retirer" card and change this reserve amount.
- Non-admin users still see the card and computed value, but cannot modify the reserve.
- Updated cash calculations across Operations, Cash Register history, exports, and Dashboard to use the configured reserve amount.
- Rebuilt frontend dist files.
