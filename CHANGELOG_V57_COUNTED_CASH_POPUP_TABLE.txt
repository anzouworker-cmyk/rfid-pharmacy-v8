V57 - Counted cash popup table

Changes:
- In the Operations page, clicking the card "S. caisse (compté)" now opens a popup window.
- The popup contains a small 2-column table:

- This means the popup and the "Monnaie stock" table stay synchronized.
- Added a total counted amount at the bottom of the popup.
- The total counted amount is also displayed on the card "S. caisse (compté)".
- Rebuilt frontend dist files.
