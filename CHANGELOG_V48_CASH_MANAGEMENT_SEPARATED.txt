V48 - Cash management operations/results separation

Changes:
- In "Gestion de caisse", separated the content into 3 blocks:
  1. Opérations de caisse
  2. Résultats de caisse
  3. Cash register history
- S. caisse (compté) now comes automatically from the total of "Monnaie stock".
- À retirer is now auto-calculated as: S. caisse (compté) - 2000 DH if positive.
- Remboursements field removed from the main management table.
- Results section now shows calculated values separately:
  - Dépenses enregistrées
  - Tot. vente
  - C. fermeture (calculé)
  - Montant manquant
  - Montant surplus
  - Écart fermeture réel vs calculée
- Updated export logic to reflect the new structure.
- Rebuilt frontend dist files.
