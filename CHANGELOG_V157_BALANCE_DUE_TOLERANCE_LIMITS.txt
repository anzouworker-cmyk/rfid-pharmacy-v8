V157 - Balance due and tolerance limits

- Updated Dashboard Caisse "Balance due progress" logic.
- Replaced the old balanced-days progress with a cash-gap tolerance progress.
- Added configurable tolerance limit in DH for cash gap progression.
- Balance due now represents the daily cash gap:
  - surplus => positive amount (+) to withdraw from cash
  - shortage => negative amount (-) to add to cash
  - zero => no action required
- Added a descriptive action title under the Balance due value.
- Added configurable monthly expense limit in DH.
- Tot. dépenses progress is now based on monthly expenses / monthly expense limit instead of expenses / sales.
- Added limit edit controls in Dashboard Caisse.
- Added settings entries in Operations for tolerance limit and monthly expense limit.
- Kept menu and footer unchanged.
- Frontend build tested successfully.
